Ingredient Substitutes

Baking

  • No eggs? → Use mashed banana, applesauce, or flaxseed meal + water.
  • No buttermilk? → Mix milk with lemon juice or vinegar (1 cup milk + 1 tbsp acid).
  • No baking powder? → Use ¼ tsp baking soda + ½ tsp cream of tartar per tsp of baking powder.

Cooking

  • No heavy cream? → Use milk + butter or coconut milk.
  • No soy sauce? → Try coconut aminos or Worcestershire sauce.
  • No breadcrumbs? → Use crushed crackers, oats, or cornflakes.
