Herb Grilled Chicken with Roasted Vegetables

A delicious and healthy meal perfect for any occasion

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 25 minutes
Servings: 4
Difficulty: Medium
★★★★★ (4.8 from 156 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (about 6 oz each)
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, chopped into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 medium zucchini, sliced into half-moons
  • 1 medium yellow squash, sliced into half-moons
  • 1 red onion, cut into wedges
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together 2 tablespoons olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, rosemary, thyme, oregano, 1 teaspoon salt, and 1/2 teaspoon black pepper.

  2. Add chicken breasts to the marinade, turning to coat evenly. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 4 hours.

  3. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Preheat grill to medium-high heat.

  4. On a large baking sheet, toss bell peppers, zucchini, yellow squash, red onion, and cherry tomatoes with remaining 1 tablespoon olive oil. Season with salt and pepper.

  5. Roast vegetables in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, stirring halfway through, until tender and slightly charred.

  6. While vegetables are roasting, remove chicken from marinade and grill for 5-7 minutes per side, or until internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).

  7. Let chicken rest for 5 minutes before slicing.

  8. Serve sliced chicken over roasted vegetables, garnished with fresh parsley.

Chef's Tips

For extra flavor, add a splash of balsamic vinegar to the vegetables before roasting. You can also substitute chicken thighs for the breasts if you prefer darker meat.
