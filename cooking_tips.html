Cooking Tips & Tricks

  • Season in Layers: Add salt and spices gradually while cooking to build flavor.
  • Let Meat Rest: Allow cooked meat to rest for a few minutes before cutting to keep it juicy.
  • Use Room Temperature Ingredients: For better baking results, use room-temperature eggs and butter.
  • Don’t Overcrowd the Pan: Give food space when frying or roasting to ensure even cooking.
  • Revive Stale Bread: Sprinkle water on stale bread and bake at 350°F (175°C) for a few minutes.
  • Sharp Knives Are Safer: A sharp knife requires less force, reducing the risk of accidents.
  • Taste as You Cook: Adjust seasoning as needed rather than waiting until the end.
