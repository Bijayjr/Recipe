Food Trends & Nutrition Guides (2025)

Trending Foods

  • Plant-Based Protein: Lentils, chickpeas, and jackfruit as meat alternatives.
  • Fermented Foods: Kimchi, kombucha, and miso for gut health.
  • Alternative Flours: Almond, coconut, and chickpea flour for gluten-free diets.
  • Seaweed Snacks: Rich in iodine and a sustainable food source.
  • Mushroom Coffee: A low-acid alternative with health benefits.

Nutrition Guides

  • Increase Fiber: Whole grains, beans, and vegetables help digestion and gut health.
  • Healthy Fats: Avocados, nuts, and olive oil support brain function.
  • Limit Processed Sugars: Opt for natural sweeteners like honey or dates.
  • Hydration Tips: Infuse water with fruit or herbs for a tastier way to drink more.
