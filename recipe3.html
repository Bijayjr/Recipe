Lemon Garlic Shrimp Risotto

A zesty and creamy shrimp risotto with garlic and lemon

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 30 minutes
Servings: 4
Difficulty: Medium
★★★★☆ (4.5 from 85 ratings)

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 1/2 cups Arborio rice
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Directions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add shrimp and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, until pink and cooked through. Remove shrimp from the skillet and set aside.

  2. In the same skillet, add onion and garlic. Cook for 3-4 minutes, until softened and fragrant.

  3. Add Arborio rice and stir to coat with the oil, cooking for 1-2 minutes.

  4. Pour in white wine and stir until mostly absorbed by the rice.

  5. Slowly add chicken broth, one ladle at a time, stirring continuously until absorbed before adding more broth. Continue this process until rice is tender and creamy (about 18-20 minutes).

  6. Stir in Parmesan cheese, butter, lemon juice, lemon zest, and cooked shrimp. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Serve immediately, garnished with parsley and extra lemon zest if desired.

Chef's Tips

For an extra burst of flavor, add a pinch of red pepper flakes when cooking the shrimp. Use homemade or low-sodium broth for a lighter version.
